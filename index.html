Evidence 1 · Stakeholder research
Current products are not helping obstetric teams make better choices

Across interviews with nurses, midwives, registrars, and O&G consultants, four systemic failure modes emerged: difficulty differentiating alerts objectively, hours of waveform overload, missed subtle anomalies, and interpretation disagreements at escalation handoffs.

Evidence 2 · Published literature
Even the best published AI models barely outperform a clinician

A Google study (2025) benchmarked multiple CTG model configurations for fetal pH prediction. Their strongest model (FHR + UC + metadata) achieved AUC 0.69 with sensitivity 0.44 at 90% specificity. Clinician performance at the same operating point: sensitivity 0.27 — a gap of only 17 percentage points.
